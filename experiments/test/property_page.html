Property Information

Rydges Sydney Central have 271 spacious guest rooms with complimentary Wi-Fi and comfortable beds to ensure you sleep sound and comfortable. But if youre a night owl, we also have you covered with Bar Surry Hills and Albies Restaurant. Our menus feature fresh seasonal fare with rich flavour packed into every bite plus our own draft beers. Or enjoy the convenience of being located in the centre of a world class city that never sleeps.

Maintenance Notice:

During the period of 7-20 October, the pool & sauna facilities will be closed for necessary maintenance. We apologise for any inconvenience.

Check-in time starts at 2pm. Check-out time is 11am.
